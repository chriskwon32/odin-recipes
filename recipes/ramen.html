Beef Miso Ramen

Description

This savory miso ramen bowl is packed with mouthwatering flavors that will delight your taste buds! If you're craving Japanese cuisine, this is the ideal dish for you. Customize it with a hard-boiled egg, mushrooms, spicy chili oil, or any toppings you prefer.

Ingredients

  • Beef sirloin steak
  • Tamari (soy sauce)
  • Coconut oil
  • Beef broth
  • Miso Paste
  • Minced Garlic
  • Sesame Oil
  • Ramen noodles
  • salt and ground pepper

Steps

  1. Combine Steak and Soy sauce, marinate 2 hours
  2. Remove steak from marinade, shake off excess
  3. Cook medium rare
  4. When steak is resting, combine broth, miso paste, garlic, sesame oil in sauce pan.
  5. Bring to boil , add ramen
  6. Serve ramen
