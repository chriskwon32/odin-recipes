Homemade Lasagna Recipe

Description

Here is my mom's unique homemade lasagna recipe, featuring a rich, meaty tomato sauce made from scratch and a savory, cheesy filling. While traditional lasagna typically uses ricotta, my mom's version combines small-curd cottage cheese with Parmesan for a delicious twist. It's the best I've ever tasted. Pair it with a fresh leafy green salad and some crusty garlic bread for a perfect meal.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Fresh Herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
