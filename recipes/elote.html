Mexican Corn on the Cob (Elote)

Description

Elote is a delicious Mexican treat featuring grilled corn on the cob coated in butter, mayonnaise, and crumbled cotija cheese. A Mexican friend shared this recipe with me, and it's a popular street food in their country.

Ingredients

  • Corn
  • Butter
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cheese
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Preheat grill medium-heat
  3. Grill until hot and lightly charred , 7-10 mins
  4. Roll corn in melted butter
  5. Serve with lime wedges
