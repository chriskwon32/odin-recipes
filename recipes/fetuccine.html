Steps

  1. gather ingredients
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. coat chicken in flour
  4. cook chicken 165degree Farenheit about 8 minutes
  5. stirfry mushrooms, marsala wine, chicken stock, baby spinach
  6. add pasta, and keep warm
